Our Story

WE ARE
HOT BEANS.

Founded in 2012, Hot Beans Web is a UK-based digital studio obsessed with building exceptional web experiences. We're not a faceless corporation — we're a tight-knit team of developers, designers, and digital strategists who genuinely love what we do.

Company Profile

WHO WE
REALLY ARE

Hot Beans Web was born out of a shared frustration — too many agencies building mediocre websites for maximum profit. Our founders, two university friends with a laptop and a rented desk, decided to do things differently.

Today we're a team of 18 people working across web design, development, digital strategy and content. We work with start-ups, SMEs and established brands across the UK and Europe, helping them build digital products that actually work — and actually look great.

Our culture is built on continuous learning. We actively recruit talented people who are new to the industry and invest heavily in training them up. Several of our senior developers started as trainees right here.

2012
Founded in a Shared Office

Two developers, one shared desk, and a big idea. Hot Beans Web opens its doors with its first client — a local restaurant needing a website.

2015
First Major Enterprise Client

Landed our first big contract with a national retail chain. Team grows to 6 people and we move into a proper studio.

2018
Trainee Programme Launched

We formalise our junior hire programme, bringing in developers fresh from courses and qualifications. Four trainees hired in year one.

2021
18-Strong Team & Going Strong

200+ projects delivered. Clients across the UK, France, and Germany. Still obsessed with quality, still hiring brilliant new talent.
