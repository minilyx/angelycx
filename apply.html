What We Look For
  • A portfolio — real projects, however small
  • Genuine curiosity — you read docs for fun
  • A growth mindset — you embrace feedback
  • Communication — you can explain your thinking
  • Relevant qualification or demonstrable self-teaching
  • HTML, CSS & JavaScript fundamentals at a minimum
Our Hiring Process
1
Application Review

A real developer reads your application. No automated filters. Takes up to 5 working days.

2
Intro Call (30 min)

A relaxed video call with one of our team. We want to know who you are, not just what you've built.

3
Technical Task

A small, take-home challenge. Nothing ridiculous — we respect your time. Max 2–3 hours.

4
Team Interview

Meet more of the team. Walk through your technical task, ask us anything, see the studio.

5
Offer

If we're both excited, we'll make you an offer. Simple as that.
